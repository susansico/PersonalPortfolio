About

I am a hi-tech professional, now a Full Stack Developer, in the San Francisco Bay Area excited about finding an opportunity to develop websites and/or web applications.

After receiving a Masters in Computer Science, I worked as a software engineer for nine years programming in C and then as a Technical Account Manager for 13 years. After taking 3 years off to travel and spend time with my family on the east coast, I found I needed more technical expertise for most technical account management positions. This led me to take HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python courses at UCSC Extension. I found I loved creating web pages and the challenge of learning new technologies. Wanting to learn more, I enrolled in a 12-week bootcamp for Full Stack Developer using the MEAN Stack. I finished the bootcamp in November 2016.
